Throughout my academic and professional career, I have been involved in the development of several nanosatellite missions, ranging from technology demonstration CubeSats to operational space systems. Most of these projects involved my participation throughout the entire development lifecycle, including mission conception, systems engineering, embedded hardware and software design, subsystem integration, environmental testing, and mission validation.

These projects have provided hands-on experience in embedded systems, spacecraft engineering, software-defined radio, satellite communications, and systems integration, contributing to the development of reliable and cost-effective space technologies.
